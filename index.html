About the Artist

I'm Kol Tregaskes, a creative explorer at the intersection of art and artificial intelligence. Through tools like Midjourney, I craft cinematic portraits, dark fantasy scenes, and cyberpunk visions that blur the line between imagination and reality.

Every piece in this gallery represents a collaboration between human creativity and AI capability - prompts refined through countless iterations, seeking that perfect moment where technology meets artistic vision.
